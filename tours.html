Sailing tours

Sailing tours are a popular form of tourism that allows individuals or groups to explore various destinations by boat. These tours typically involve sailing on a yacht, catamaran, or other types of sailboats, and can range from a few hours to several days or even weeks.

Climbing tours

Climbing tours are adventurous and thrilling experiences that involve scaling mountains, cliffs, or other natural rock formations. These tours are designed for individuals who have a passion for outdoor activities and seek a challenge in their travels.

Skiing tours

Skiing tours are organized trips that allow individuals or groups to explore and enjoy various ski resorts and slopes around the world. These tours typically include a combination of skiing or snowboarding activities, accommodation, transportation, and sometimes even meals and equipment rental.

Hiking tours

Hiking tours are organized trips that allow individuals or groups to explore and experience the beauty of nature by walking through various trails and terrains. These tours are designed for people who enjoy outdoor activities and want to immerse themselves in the natural environment.

Extreme tours

Extreme tours are a type of tourism that offers unique and exciting adventures for lovers of extreme sensations. These tours are designed for those who are looking for adrenaline, new facets and unforgettable experiences.

Rural tours

Rural tours, also known as countryside tours or rural retreats, are travel experiences that focus on exploring and immersing oneself in rural areas and their unique culture, landscapes, and way of life. These tours offer a break from the hustle and bustle of city life and provide an opportunity to connect with nature.

Jungle tours

Jungle tours are a type of tourism that provides an opportunity to experience the wildlife of the tropical jungle. This is a journey to remote places where you can meet wild animals, plants and enjoy incredible landscapes.

Geo tours

Geo Tours is a company specializing in organizing excursions and tours related to geography and nature. They offer unique and exciting travel experiences that allow participants to experience a variety of geographic locations and natural attractions.
